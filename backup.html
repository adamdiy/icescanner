Price comparison

This widget allows you to fetch the price and promo for any product and any store and display in on your web page. Clicking on the retailer's logo will take you to the product page on mySupermarket for that retailer.
The "msdata" variable specifies the mySupermarket ID of the product. When integrating please ask mySupermarket for a dictionary of products and IDs to help with your integration.
