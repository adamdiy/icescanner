What is icescanner?

Icescanner is a price comparison service for ice cream.

Use icescanner to:
- find the cheapest place for your fav ice cream
- find the best deal at one store nearby
- find the best ice cream deals of the moment

Coded by @adamdiy *FREE* education by Founders & Coders
